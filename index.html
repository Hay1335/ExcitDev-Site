Server Rules

  • #1No griefing, stealing from, or destroying other players' builds.
  • #2No hacking, X-ray, or cheats. Performance mods like Sodium/Optifine are fine.
  • #3Be respectful. No harassment, hate speech, or excessive profanity in chat.
  • #4No spam or advertising other servers.
  • #5Report bugs to staff rather than exploiting them.
  • #6Staff have final say. If you disagree, open a help ticket instead of arguing in chat.
